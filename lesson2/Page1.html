A little about me

Hi there! My name is Eika Nishimura. I am 24 years old, born and raised in New Jersey, and now a transplant living in Minnesota. I found an interest in technology early on in my life since by dad is a computer engineer. I moved in with my oldest sister a couple of years ago who was also a software programmer, but switched careers to a UX designer. After seeing my sister pursue her dreams of being in tech, and feeling tired of retail, I decided to major in computer science. This is my second course related to computer science.

When I am not in school, I am most likely working at my local grocery store. I work in the meat & seafood department and consider myself to be a connoisseur in the field. I have been working there for almost two years now.

I have a passion for bouldering, a type of climbing where no gear is required. I aspire to become a pro climber one day. I have a membership at Minneapolis Bouldering Project, which has a large but tight knit community. I usually climb up three times a week with my boyfriend. On Wednesdays, I attend a BIPOC climbing crew where we bond over our love for climbing.
