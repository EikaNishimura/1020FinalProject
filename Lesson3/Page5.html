How to pick a planter for your plant

Pick a pot or planter that has drainage. You don't want to end up soaking your soil and it not being able to dry out. You could also add pebbles to the bottom of your planter if it doesn't have a drainage hole on the bottom. I would also suggest going for a porous planter such as a terracotta which has a rustic look to it. Ceramic pots are great, too. Choose a pot that is 1-2 inches larger than the current size of the plant. If your plant is as big as your pot, it's time to repot!
